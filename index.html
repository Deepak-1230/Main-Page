SPARK INTELLIGENTIA - The MRU's Symposium

The pace of progress in the field of Science & Technology in this millennium has overshadowed the speed of Technological changes in the past decades. Technical Symposiums like Spark intelligence are an effort to catch up with the pace of technological innovation.

SPARK-INTELLIGENTIA is a technical festival. The event seeks to achieve communication of innovative ideas that promote the cause of the technological advantage among the students and also to keep them abreast with the latest advances in their respective fields. It also opens a window to the participants to interact with people from different backgrounds and upgrade their knowledge.

The theme "SPARK-INTELLIGENTIA" asserts the fact that the future of technology lies in the hands of the youth intelligents. This is the time for the students to draw out their talents in the areas related to academic routine.

Code Hackathon

A programming contest is pleasurable for a budding programmer. It is a delightful playground where programmers can explore their intelligence. “It is not the place for the frightened lizards but for the Programming Wizards.” It provides the perfect platform for all the programmers to showcase their skills. Get down on the battle ground and claim the right of being the "Best Programmer".

First Round : Online athttp://www.codechef.com/ACMN2015/
Second Round : Campus

Online Programing Contest Details

First Round: Online round. This round is open to all. Participants will be given certain questions and they would have to code the solutions. The problems will test your ingenuity, insight and your ability to optimize. All you need is a favourite text editor, a basic knowledge of C, C++/JAVA and the satisfaction of seeing your code work first time..

Second Round: This round is on campus event. The participants shortlisted from the first round will have to come down to Vasavi Campus for the second round. This round has some debugging questions where the team will be given an erroneous code and they will have to remove all the errors to produce the desired output.
Also other questions where the team has to produce the desired output given a code snippet. The team solving these questions correctly in minimum time wins!!

Each team can hold two participants. The participation for the first round is free. The winner gets a merit certificate and prize money. Participation certificates will be awarded to all attending Round 2 at Vasavi College of Engineering.

Paper Presentation

As the popular adage goes “Exchanging ideas alone will leave you with more than what you start with; and exchanging everything else, will leave you with the same”. We, here at demo strive to make it true. So for all those tech savvy folks brimming with ideas, bubbling with confidence and having the right College, your wait for the right opportunity to present your ideas to your fellow tech community is finally over.

Paper presentation event of College CSFest'15 is the best platform to place your ideas and solutions towards the latest and emerging technologies.

Paper Presentation Details

Rules

  • Registration fee is Rs.200 only.
  • No paper will be taken in the last 2 days to the fest.
  • A student can give only one paper. (Candidates however can submit more than one paper by paying the requisite amount for each paper but are allowed to present only one among them if selected)
  • Maximum of 2 participants per group.
  • Failing to be present when called in presentation will result in disqualification.
  • No other topics apart from the topic opted will be entertained.
  • Maximum 10 minute presentation.(8 minutes of talk followed by 2 minutes of questionnaire)
  • Paper submitted should be restricted to 3 single sided A4 pages in IEEE format.
  • Submit your papers to your email goes here
    with name, ph.no, mail-id, college name and Receipt No. (Your bank transaction no. for online applicants)
  • Last date for submission is 4th March,2015
  • All the submitted candidates will be given Certificate of Participation; Cash price and Merit certificates for winners

Singing & Band

"Code hard.Party hard"- a tag line for software geeks. This is the event that matches up with our geeks.Lets us race with the remote cars in accordance with the passion for technology.

Singing & Band Details

In this event the participant has to reach the end of the maze by crossing all the obstacles from the starting point. The main criteria here is that the participant should reach the destination with the help of remote cars. They have to control the cars. In the path from start to end, there will be obstacles and for crossing an obstacle the participant has to answer the question. It will be a simultaneous game by two players for two mazes.

Haunted House

In this event, contestants prepare a poster that showcases and effectively presents experiments with given objectives, new ideas related to the field. The main idea of the event is to analyze a topic and think with a new bent of mind. The more creative you get, the higher is your chance of impressing the panel.

Hanunted House Details

First round - Decide a theme from the given themes and the participants have to submit the poster abstracts along with the images (maximum of 3 images) describing your theme and submit to the specified mail id. The best posters will be shortlisted for the second round.

Second round - Shortlisted people have to give a presentation of their selected abstract on the day of event, on a specified dimension paper (can be A4, A1 charts or posters).

Rules
• Registration fee is Rs.150 only
• No paper will be taken in the last 2 days to the fest.
• Participants can submit their abstract by contacting the organizers/volunteers in their nearest area from the Nodal Centers option provided.
• Papers shortlisted will be notified through mail/sms on 5th march 2015.
• A student can give only one paper. (Candidates however can submit more than one paper by paying the requisite amount for each paper but are allowed to present only one among them if selected)
• Maximum of 2 participants per group.
• Failing to be present when called in presentation will result in disqualification.
• No other topics apart from the topic opted will be entertained.
• Maximum 15 minute presentation.(13 minutes of talk followed by 2 minutes of questionnaire)
• Submit your papers to poster@vce-csfest.in with name,ph.no,mail-id,college name.
• Last date for submission is 4th March,2015
• All the submitted candidates will be given Certificate of Participation; Cash price and Merit certificates for winners
